# Platform: Magnol.ai

Much of the work showcased here is related to **[Magnol.ai](https://www.magnolai.lilly.com)**, an industry award-winning Digital Health Technology (DHT) platform designed for modern clinical research.

Magnol.ai enables:

* Continuous ingestion of large-scale **wearable and digital device data**
* Automated **data quality monitoring and signal processing**
* Transformation of raw sensor streams into **digital measures and biomarkers**
* Visualization and exploration tools for clinical scientists
* **AI-assisted insight generation and no-code data analysis**

The platform supports **connected clinical trials**, enabling continuous monitoring of patient physiology and behavior outside traditional clinical settings.

## Translational Technology Applications

This GitHub also includes examples of **translational technology use cases**, where digital sensing, AI, and data platform capabilities are applied beyond traditional clinical trials.

Examples include:

* **Continuous safety monitoring in TZP Type 1 Diabetes (T1D) trials**, leveraging wearable sensor data to support real-time safety oversight and digital endpoint analysis.

* **Situational awareness systems for manufacturing environments**, including solutions deployed on Lilly’s parenteral production floors that analyze worker movement patterns (e.g., walking speed tracking) to improve operational efficiency and safety.

* **Document intelligence systems powered by Digital Worker Equivalents (DWE)**, which apply AI to automate document understanding and knowledge extraction to accelerate product research and development workflows.

These projects demonstrate how digital health platform capabilities can translate into **operational intelligence, safety monitoring, and AI-driven knowledge systems** across clinical and manufacturing environments.
